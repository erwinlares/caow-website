Capital Aikikai of Wisconsin

A dojo dedicated to the practice and promotion of the Japanese Martial Art of Aikido. We are a 501(c)(3) nonprofit affiliated with the Capital Aikido Federation.

Capital Aikikai of Wisconsin was able to open its doors thanks to a generous donation from the Ikigai Foundation. The Ikigai Foundation supports work that fosters joy, purpose, and meaning — values at the heart of Aikido. We're proud to continue this vision by offering a space for all to grow and connect through practice.
